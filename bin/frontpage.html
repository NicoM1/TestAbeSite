slickrock.io is:

simple.
  • no username, no password.
  • no room setup, just find something unclaimed and take it for yourself.
private.
  • lock rooms with a password if you wish, unlock whenever you want.
modern.
  • designed to look clean on every surface, slickrock.io scales gracefully with screen size.
embeddable
  • want a simple chat embedded in your website? slickrock.io works great for game jams, forums, and more.
